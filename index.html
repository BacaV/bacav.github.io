FAQ

What devices is the app compatible with?

Our app is available on both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play. It also syncs seamlessly with popular wearables like Apple Watch, Fitbit, and Google Fit.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your app settings or directly via your App Store/Google Play account. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the billing cycle.

Do I need equipment for the workout plans?

Not necessarily! Our app offers both equipment-free workouts and routines that use basic gym equipment. You can choose plans that fit your preference, whether you're working out at home or the gym.

Is there a free version of the app?

Yes! We offer a free plan that includes basic workout routines, progress tracking, and access to our community. For more advanced features like personalized plans and live coaching, you can upgrade to our Premium or Pro plans.
